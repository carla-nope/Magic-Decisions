What is this guide?

You're making hundreds of decisions every day. Most of them are tiny, temporary, and don't really matter. But they still eat up mental energy that could be spent on things you actually care about.


This guide lists 100 small decisions that most families waste time debating. For each one, we've included a simple "stop here" insight showing why you can just... move on. No overthinking needed.

How to Use This Guide

  1. Print it out - Keep it on the fridge or in a family command center
  2. Use it during decision moments - When someone gets stuck, find the relevant decision and point to the "stop here" box
  3. Train your brain - Over time, you'll naturally stop overthinking these choices without needing the guide
  4. Share with your kids - Help them see which decisions deserve energy and which don't
